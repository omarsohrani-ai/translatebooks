✦ Best practices
Use batch size 3
Best balance of speed and reliability. Batch 4 risks hitting the 6,000-token-per-minute input ceiling on dense pages.
Translate in chapters
Use Start/End page to translate 50–80 pages at a time. Pause between sessions to conserve daily quota.
Quota resets at midnight PT
Pacific Time (UTC-7/8). Plan heavy jobs for just after midnight to get a full fresh 1,000 requests (flash-lite) or 20 (flash).
Two speed modes
Flash-lite (active): 1,000 RPD — translate a full 400-page book in one session. Flash: only 20 RPD free but richer literary output — enable billing for ~$0.20/book.
Stop & download anytime
Hit Stop and download a partial PDF. Come back and set Start Page to where you left off — no progress is lost.
Enable billing for $0
Adding a card at aistudio.google.com unlocks Tier 1 (higher limits) with no charge unless you exceed free amounts.
⚠ Free tier data may be used by Google to improve their models. For private or sensitive documents, enable billing to opt out.
